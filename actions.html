Actions You Can Take

You’ve already started! Thank you for taking the time to get better informed about the district’s budget, and for sharing your own thoughts and values to help guide future decisions. If you would like to do more, here are some possibilities:

Register to vote.

Follow current developments

  • DJUSD Board of Education meeting calendar and online agenda
  • Watch DJUSD Board of Education meetings live and in replay at www.djusd.tv
  • Learn more about the DJUSD Local Control Accountability Plan

Share your views

  • Send your questions and suggestions on the Local Control and Accountability Plan to: LCAP@djusd.net
  • Send your questions or comments to the Superintendent: superintendent@djusd.net
  • Send your questions or comments to the Board of Education: boe@djusd.net
  • Send your questions or comments to your state elected representatives:
    • Assembly Member Bill Dodd: Bill.Dodd@asm.ca.gov
    • Senator Lois Wolk: Lois.Wolk@sen.ca.gov
    • Governor Jerry Brown: http://govnews.ca.gov/gov39mail/mail.php
